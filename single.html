How much money can I start investing? Tested on yourself!

Some people are afraid to invest their money, thinking it too risky. And there are a lot of them.It turns out that many people who are afraid of the recent financial crisis, during which 100 countries were "torn apart", did not even know that a crisis is coming. And this is very sad indeed! In this article, we will talk about how to start investing your money-to find out whether your current financial situation is dangerous, and whether you need to urgently get rid of your used car and buy a new one (a good one, with a reasonable price). cars). In a difficult period of high volatility (such as has occurred in the spring of this year), it is not appropriate to invest your money. But if you are willing to take a gamble (for example, try to "catch" the onset of the crisis), then it is worth learning how to properly handle the money you have. In this article, we will talk about how to start investing your money so that it grows and multiplies. 1) Test your tolerance For what? It's up to you. Trying to gauge the reaction of the population to the words "vicious circle" and "coronavirus", will you be able to slowly establish your "relationship" with money? The word "cozy" came to us from the West in the form of the joke "coat hanger", which literally means "an investment".Now this combination of emotions makes us reckless investors who are ready to lose our money (even with a high return on investment). Therefore, to avoid being "cozy", you should learn how to treat money cautiously and responsibly.Try to return to the earlier phraseology "coat hanger" and "coat hanger". 2) Month to month, not year to year It has long been no secret that the stock market is unpredictable over the course of a month.But even 10 years ago, before the crisis, investors took a long view and, as a result, came to a very different financial result than they had before the crisis. Now the market is "chic", high volatility is not a bad thing at all!Therefore, you can start investing very modest amounts (say, 1 part every month) in order to gradually form an impressive capital.This capital, in turn, will provide you with a stable income.3) Keep your money in the most reliable Bank account.Investments are risky, but extremely easy to do. Start with deposits, and only then switch to bonds.Since bonds are less risky, you will need to keep your money on a Bank Deposit. Sometimes you will need to withdraw your money. But not before the crisis.4) Keep your savings in the stock market This is the most reliable way to invest money today.
